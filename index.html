A free qualitative research tool
Perform Thematic Analysis, Content Analysis, and Narrative Analysis on your interview transcripts, field notes, and documents. Results are saved locally in your browser — your data never leaves your device.

Configure a key → Add documents → Analyse

1
Open Google AI Studio

3
